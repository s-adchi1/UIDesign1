What is This?

Punctuality is a necessity in ever SNU class, and a lot of students commute from far locations to attend such classes. This website serves as a public utility that gives students specific directions to get to their first class right from their home. It makes it easier to plan routes to even bicycle stations or cafaterias in case you want to make a pit stop before class starts.
